Numbers and facts about the Swedish fashion and textile industry









In the year of 2014, the turnover for the Swedish fashion industry was 264 billion SEK in total. The export vs. sales on the Swedish market was thereby 65 % vs. 35%, which equals 173 billion SEK and 91 billion SEK.



















The fashion industry is overall a women dominated industry with 73% women as employees and 27% men in the year of 2013, mostly within retail.

But in the level of board members and executives, there are still more men. However, there are more women in these positions within the fashion industry than in general in Sweden. If we look at only executives, the share of women are 29% in the fashion industry (Chart) vs. 15% in general.

Board members equals 60% men and 40% women in the fashion industry, compared to 69% men and 31% women in general in Sweden.















Source: The Swedish Textile & Fashion company
